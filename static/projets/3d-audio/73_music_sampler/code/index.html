Music Sampler

How to use it?

Just move around the sphere and click to play a sound. You can choose between 3 instruments and 2 types of percussions.
You can also play with your keyboard! Use A,Z,E,R,T,Y to play. (you can play multiple notes)
By moving your mouse to the left and right, you can play with filters. Try it out!
